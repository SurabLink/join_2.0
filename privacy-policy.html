Privacy Policy

We take the protection of your personal data very seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use and protect your information when using the Join Kanban Project Management Tool.

1. Data Collection

We only collect data that is necessary for the functionality of the application. This includes task data, contact information and usage-related data stored locally in your browser.

2. Data Storage

All data is stored locally using your browser’s local storage. No personal data is transmitted to external servers.

3. Purpose of Data Processing

The collected data is used solely to provide and improve the functionality of the application.

4. Third-Party Services

This application does not use third-party tracking tools or external analytics services.

5. User Rights

You have the right to access, modify or delete your data at any time by clearing your browser’s local storage.

6. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to update this Privacy Policy if necessary to reflect changes in the application.

7. Contact

If you have any questions regarding data protection, please contact the project administrator.
